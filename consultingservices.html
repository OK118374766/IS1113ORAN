Personal Introduction to Consulting Services

I was first introduced to the Information Technology Consultancy realm in Transition Year. I was lucky enough to undertake my work experience in BlueFin Payment Systems, a local IT firm. Though upon my arrival I was unfamiliar with many of the technologies utilised in the firm, my supervisor (the regional manager) gave me great insight into these technologies during my time there.
My supervisor informed me that she was also an IT consultant. When I expressed an interest in learning more about IT consultancy, she informed me of the skills that an IT consultant needs to possess, namely :

  • Advisory skills
  • Technical skills
  • Business skills
  • Communication skills
  • Management skills
  • Advisory language skills
  • Business and management language skills
  • Technical language skills

Interest

As mentioned previously, my time at BlueFin Payment Systems succeeded in introducing me to the IT consultancy realm. I have great interest in this field, perhaps as a result of the fond memories that I have of giving guitar lessons when I was in school.
When applying for the CAO, I was unsure as to whether to put Business Information Systems or Education as my first choice, hence, IT Consultancy represents an enticing choice that combines these two interests of mine.
